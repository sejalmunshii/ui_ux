Blockquote, Details, and Summary Tags in HTML

1. Blockquote (`
`)

The <blockquote> tag is used to define a section that is quoted from another source.

<blockquote>
    "The only limit to our realization of tomorrow will be our doubts of today."
    <cite>- Franklin D. Roosevelt</cite>
</blockquote>
"The only limit to our realization of tomorrow will be our doubts of today." - Franklin D. Roosevelt

2. Details (`
`)

The <details> tag is used to create a disclosure widget that shows additional information.

<details>
    <summary>More Information</summary>
    <p>This section contains additional details that can be expanded or collapsed.</p>
</details>
More Information

This section contains additional details that can be expanded or collapsed.

3. Summary (``)

The <summary> tag is used as a heading for the <details> tag.

<details>
    <summary>Click to reveal details</summary>
    <p>Details about the topic go here. This content is hidden until the summary is clicked.</p>
</details>
Click to reveal details

Details about the topic go here. This content is hidden until the summary is clicked.
